# customer-churn-analysis-dashboard

Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using Excel, SQL, and Tableau to identify key drivers of customer retention and revenue risk.

🛠 Tools Used

Excel (Data Cleaning)

SQL (Data Analysis)

Tableau (Data Visualization)

📈 Key Insights

Customers with international plans have a 3x higher churn rate (~41.6%)

Customers with voice mail plans show lower churn (~12.2%)

Overall churn rate is 17.7%

💡 Business Recommendations

Improve international plan pricing and service quality

Promote bundled services (voice mail plans)

Target high-risk customers with retention strategies

Customers without voice mail plans show a higher proportion of churn compared to those with voice mail.

International plan customers have a significantly higher churn proportion.
